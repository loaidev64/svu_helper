## 3. Course General Objectives:  

The objective of the course is to introduce the most known network applications that have been created during the last 40 years and became very popular and commonly used in our day to day life. From these applications, we can recall Mail transfer protocols, Web and HTTP protocol, P2P protocols, File transfer protocol, Domain Name Systems, and Network management protocol.
